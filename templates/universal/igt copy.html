Incoming Global Talent

What was the visa procedure for our EPs before 2017?

Any person who is coming to India for internship was subjected to 3 types of visa:
  • Student Visa (for students, short term internships),
  • Entry Visa (non-paid short term internships)
  • Employment Visa (for anyone for any paid internship or job and the salary requirement is 25000USD per annum).
 
In this case, AIESEC had a privilege for our EPs. All our EPs were supposed to get Business or Entry visa.
It was stated in the explanation of Business Visa: mha1.nic.in/pdfs/ForeigD-work_visa_faq.pdf
 
In this way, everyone was able to get visa through AIESEC regardless of the amount of salary.
 
Yet, there are a couple of changes regarding to this policy.

Timeline of Visa Procedure Change

There were a couple of articles stating that government will launch new visa types to ease the process of getting foreign interns. You can find those articles above.

The reason we received is as follows: "There's a new policy for visa, you monthly salary needs to be 65k per month"

Still, there was no official information in internet regarding to change in visa policies. So we asked MCVPs from those countries to approach to embassies and get more info about the new regulation. MCVPs could not get any response. And embassies were not picking up the calls.
 
In the mean time, thanks to Akshay, we started approaching some advisors to help us to get an appointment with MHA so we could understand the new policy and try to position AIESEC in a way to preserve our privileges.

INTERN VISA REQUIREMENTS

INTERN VISA is the new visa type that Government of India has launched. Our operations have been slowed down with the countries following this new regulation due to requirements.
Lacking of the official papers regarding to INTERN VISA, we are not able to interpret the exact way forward.
 
What are the requirements for INTERN VISA?
 

1.Visa duration would be restricted to duration of internship or 1 year, whichever is less.

 

2.Intern visa would be granted immediately after graduation/post graduation with no more than a gap of 1 year immediately after graduation/post graduation

 

3.Required documents - letter from the sponsoring Indian company/educational institution/NGO concerned clearly indicating the period of internship. Supporting letter from AIESEC and AIESEC acceptance letter. Copies of relevant educational certificates are to be submitted by the applicant.

 

4. In case of internship in a company, the foreign national being sponsored should draw a minimum remuneration of Rs. 7.80 lakhs per annum. 

 

5. Interns would be required to register with the Foreigners Regional Registration Officer (FRRO)/Foreigners Registration Officer (FRO) within 14 days of arrival in India.

 

6. Undertaking from the company/organization concerned that they will ensure departure of the foreigner on completion of the internship.

 

7. Interns are not permitted to take up employment in India immediately after completion of internship. Conversion of Intern visa to any other type of visa is not permitted.

 

8.Earnings from internship with Indian company/educational institution/NGO concerned will be subject to the Indian Income Tax Regulations.

BUSINESS and ENTRY VISA

Business Visa and Entry Visa are the types that our EPs shall apply. In most of the cases, while GT EPs get Business Visa, GE EPs get Entry Visa.

You can get more information regarding to those visa types here: 

http://support.aiesec.in/support/solutions/articles/4000007988-visa-information-for-india

 

There's no salary or student/graduation status requirements for Business and Entry visas.

About E-Business Visa

According to the new policies, e-visas have been extended to 60 days and another type of e-visa has been launched: e-business visa.

 

Yet, this does not mean that this is the type of visa our EPs can apply. This visa type can be issued only for the following purposes:

  • TO SET UP INDUSTRIAL/BUSINESS VENTURE 
  • SALE/PURCHASE/TRADE  
  • ATTEND TECHNICAL/BUSINESS MEETINGS 
  • TO RECRUIT MANPOWER 
  • PARTICIPATION IN EXHIBITIONS,BUSINESS/TRADE FAIRS 
  • EXPERT/SPECIALIST IN CONNECTION WITH AN ONGOING PROJECT 
  • CONDUCTING TOURS 
  • TO DELIVER LECTURE/S UNDER GLOBAL INITIATIVE FOR ACADEMIC NETWORKS (GIAN) 

 

And in the application form, the applicant has to represent a company from his/her origin country. Also, this visa type requires the following documents:

  1. Applicant's recent colored photograph (dimentions 2in X 2in) size less than 1MB 
  2. Copy of Passport page containing personal particulars 
  3. Copy of Business Card

 

Hence, e-business visa is not applicable for AIESEC Internship purposes.

Visa for GE

According to the new intern visa policy, any person coming to intern in a private company, educational institution or NGOs in India is subjected to Intern Visa.

Regardless of being GE or GT internship that person has to apply for Intern Visa if this country already follows intern visa policy. In such sense, you EPs will not be able to get visa due to 65k salary requirement. Hence, for all your GE EPs, focus on the countries which still do issue Business or Entry Visa.

FRRO

What is FRRO?

Every Foreigner visiting India for more than 180 days is required to get registered with concerned FRRO , within 14 days of his/her first arrival, irrespective of the duration of his / her stay.
